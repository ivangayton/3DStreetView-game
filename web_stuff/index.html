Welcome to 3DStreetView!

These are immersive environments ready to explore like a 3D game. They are created by local people in low-income environments, primarily using simple equipment (locally-purchased mobile phones or consumer-grade drones) and Free Software.

In other words, everything you see here can be done without expensive hardware or software!
